The Station Vibes

Located just steps from the sand on West Beech Street, The Station for Hair brings the relaxed, breezy energy of Long Beach into the salon chair.


We ditched the stuffy salon atmosphere for something more organic. Whether you need a sun-kissed Balayage or a fresh cut to manage the salt air, we are here to make you look and feel effortless.
