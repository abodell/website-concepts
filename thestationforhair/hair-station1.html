Welcome to The Station

Since 2013

At The Station for Hair, we believe your hair is your best accessory. Located on West Beech Street, our salon offers a friendly, relaxing atmosphere where you can unwind while we work our magic.

From precision cuts to complex color corrections and Balayage, our team is dedicated to maintaining the health and integrity of your hair while achieving the look you desire.
